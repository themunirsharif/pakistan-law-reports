Bail confirmed where recovery evidence was uncorroborated

Headnote

Criminal Procedure Code, 1898 — S. 497 — Bail — Recovery of weapon not forensically linked to crime scene — Where the prosecution's case rests substantially on a recovered weapon and no forensic report connects it to the empties secured from the crime scene, continued pre-trial detention is not warranted and bail is to be confirmed, subject to surety bonds.
